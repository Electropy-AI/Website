01 / Premise
The energy transition
is becoming chaotic.

An evolving supply–demand mix. Growing renewable penetration. New capital flowing into assets exposed to weather, policy, and market volatility never priced before.

The result: emerging risks across every stakeholder in the electron's journey — from generator to consumer, from policy to grid.

Bringing order to this chaos demands a new foundation: weather modelling, operations research, power-market design, finance, and trading converging into a single intelligence layer.

That foundation is what we are building.

Four pillars where chaos becomes order.

Route‑to‑market evaluation

Forecasting & market modelling
